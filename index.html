          Local Sightseeing

          Useful Places to Visit.

          Taj Mahal Viewpoint

          Mehtab Bagh across the river offers the absolute best sunset and sunrise views without the crowd.

          • Best Time: 5:30 AM or 6:00 PM
          • Entry Fee: ₹250 (Foreigner) / ₹50 (Indian)
          • Local Tip: Bring binoculars and insect repellent.

          Shibuya Sky Deck

          A stunning open-air roof deck overlooking the world-famous Shibuya crossing and Tokyo skyline.

          • Best Time: 4:30 PM (Golden hour)
          • Entry Fee: ¥2,200 (~₹1,200)
          • Local Tip: Book tickets online 30 days in advance.

          Gardens by the Bay

          Singapore's futuristic park featuring giant supertrees and massive air-conditioned greenhouses.

          • Best Time: 7:45 PM (Light show)
          • Entry Fee: SGD 30 (~₹1,850)
          • Local Tip: The outdoor garden light show is free.
